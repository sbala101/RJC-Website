What Are Text-Sets?

A text set is an interactive document that helps members of the community become more informed and understanding. It consists of information, history, reflective questions, videos, and activities. It is meant to be both engaging and informative, and is a versatile tool to help people learn from their actions and/or educate themselves on the topic. There are a variety of different versions to each text set, all aimed at different audiences, from teachers to students. Click the pictures to visit our Text-Sets!

Student Edition:
The student edition of each text set is meant to teach students about the history and impact of a specific topic/incident. It contains activities and follow up questions (some mandatory and some optional) to help the student learn and grow while also healing themselves and the community around them throughout the process.
