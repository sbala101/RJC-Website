Meet Hailey Godin (she/her), The President

As a rising Junior at California High School, Hailey Godin discovered her interest for social justice and her love of helping her community in a social justice and community English class her freshman year. With the help of her freshman English teacher, her friends and classmates, she helped create the Cal Restorative Justice Coalition over the summer prior to her sophomore year. Outside of school, she is a dedicated travel softball player who has played for over eight years and made varsity twice. She likes to spend her free time outside, with friends and listening to music. With only two years left at Cal, Hailey hopes to make a positive difference in the community, set up Cal RJC for success following her graduation, and spark a similar change in other schools throughout the SRVUSD school district.

Meet Brooke Johnson (she/her), The Secretary

Brooke Johnson discovered her passion for social justice in her freshman year of high school, in her English justice and community class. Alongside her friends, she helped create the Cal Restorative Justice Coalition to make a difference in her school community. Outside of school, Brooke has played competitive volleyball since she was 12, and loves reading and baking. In the rest of her time at high school, she wants to create a lasting impact at Cal, and leave knowing she made a difference.
